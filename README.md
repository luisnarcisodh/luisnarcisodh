### About Me

As a UI/UX Designer with a strong foundation in Information Systems, I bridge the gap between aesthetic design and functional technology. I believe that good design is invisible—it simply works.

- 🎨 **Focus:** User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, and Design Systems.
- 💻 **Current Studies:** BS Information Systems at La Verdad Christian College - Apalit.
- 🤝 **Open for:** Internships, collaborative projects, and freelance design opportunities.

---

### Design Philosophy

> *"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." — Steve Jobs*

I approach every project with empathy for the user. By combining **Data-Driven Research** with **Creative Problem Solving**, I strive to build interfaces that don't just look visually stunning, but also provide seamless and logical user journeys.
